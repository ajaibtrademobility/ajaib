About Us

AJAIB TRADE & MOBILITY is a multi-vertical proprietorship enterprise delivering integrated trade, mobility, logistics, and fresh supply solutions. We operate with a compliance-first mindset and a long-term growth vision.

Business Verticals

Online Garments & Jewellery

Retail and wholesale of garments, footwear, and jewellery through digital channels, focusing on quality sourcing, transparent pricing, and reliable delivery.

Logistics & Premium Cab Services

Freight transport, executive cabs, airport transfers, and corporate mobility solutions with emphasis on safety, punctuality, and professional service.

Livestock Fresh Supply

Ethical trading and fresh supply of livestock, connecting rural producers directly with organized markets and bulk buyers.

Vision

To build a nationally respected trade and mobility ecosystem that bridges rural and urban markets, enables ethical commerce, and delivers reliable movement of goods and people across India.

Mission

  • Operate transparent and compliant trade platforms
  • Deliver safe, premium mobility and logistics services
  • Support livestock and fresh supply value chains
  • Create sustainable livelihoods and long-term value
